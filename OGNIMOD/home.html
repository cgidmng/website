About Us

Lovely tea was established on May of 2022 by Jhay-r Gonzales , the owner sparks the interest in milk tea when they tasted the milk teas in Taiwan. Lovely tea humbly started small in their house at suklayin with just purely milk tea in their products , then later on shawarma was added to the menu followed by the burgers, fries and nachos, then it boomed until now they have a physical store now located at near EXACT Colleges of Asia, Camba Arayat Pampanga. When the physical store was built they incorporated chicken wings and rice meals such as spam with egg and shanghai rice just to name a few in their product list. Later we will also add siomai rice for more affordable but delicious meal for the costumers to avail. The owners have picked the locations so that it will be accessible by the high school and college students and it is in front of the highway for convenience plus delivery is ready and available at working hours. Unfortunately lovely tea is not open for franchisee. We may have experiences some hurdles during the pandemic but that didn’t stop us for expanding and making our business bigger and we promise you that we prioritizes Quality over Quantity. Lovely Tea offer high quality foods to satisfy your cravings. Enjoy our tasty chicken wings, shawarma, burger and milk tea in an affordable price. Lovely Tea "A Taste of a Taiwan Milk Tea".
